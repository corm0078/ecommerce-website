About

Koral, was created in 2010 by Nathalie Jean. She was tiered of buying jewelry that would break or that was too expensive therefore she created a line of jewelry that would appeal to a young adults, with its style and prices. Koral believes in creating pieces that will stand the test of time with classic elements that still follow the modern current trends. Nathalie attended the Georgian college and graduated in the Jewelry and metals program. She is now using these skills to create the most up to date style for fashion jewelry.

Here at Koral we value quality of material therefore with every single purchase a card will be included in the package with all the materials used to create this special piece and a 1 year warranty. These pieces are very special to us and we create them with you in mind therefore we want you to be happy with the product you will be wearing.

Giving Back

Koral wants to give back to the community, we are committed to donating 10% of all sales to woman in need. You can select in what part of the world you would like to apply this contribution, from your home town to the other side of the world you get to chose where you believe this money will make the biggest difference.
